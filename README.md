
<h1>FarmConnect</h1>
Overview
FarmConnect is a digital marketplace platform designed to connect farmers directly with customers. It aims to simplify the supply chain for agricultural products by enabling farmers to list their goods and customers to browse and place orders in an accessible, user-friendly environment.

<h2>Features</h2>
Product listings with detailed descriptions and pricing
Simple order process for customers to place and track orders
Farmer profile pages to highlight seller information and product origin
Focused on accessibility and usability for rural users
